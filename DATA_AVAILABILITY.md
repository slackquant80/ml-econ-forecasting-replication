# Data availability

FRED-MD is published by the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis and combines series from multiple original providers. The official FRED-MD page describes the database as publicly accessible, but individual component series may carry provider-specific citation or redistribution conditions. For that reason this repository does not redistribute the frozen source CSV.

Reproduction uses the dated June 2026 FRED-MD file from the official page, saved locally as `data/current.csv` and verified against MD5 `8591dd9f169f7aeb45b7c91782fbd947`.

The repository does distribute researcher-generated forecast panels, accuracy summaries, statistical-test results, validation records, and manuscript table/figure exports.
